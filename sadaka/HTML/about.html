ABOUT HUMANITY FOR CHARITY INTERNATIONAL

At Humanity for Charity International, we are a Non-Govermental Organization working to transform lives across borders. While our roots and current operations are anchored in Uganda, where we work closely with the Lira City NGO Monitoring Committee, our vision extends far beyond these boundaries. !

We believe in creating lasting change that knows no geographical limits. At the heart of our mission is the empowerment of women and girls, alongside our dedication to transforming the lives of street-connected children and vulnerable communities. We champion gender equality, fight against gender-based violence (GBV), and create safe spaces where women and girls can thrive.

What drives us every day:

Empowering girls and women through education, skills training, and leadership programs Combating gender-based violence through prevention, response, and survivor support Breaking down barriers to gender equality in communities Rescuing and rehabilitating street-connected children, giving them hope and a path to a better future Supporting vulnerable children to access quality education through our sponsorship program Equipping out-of-school youth with hands-on skills in trades like tailoring, carpentry, and agriculture Strengthening women's economic independence through Village Savings and Loan Associations (VSLA) Running community education programs that challenge harmful gender norms and practices Building partnerships across borders to expand our impact globally .

We believe in the power of humanity coming together for positive change. Through our gender-responsive approach and collaboration with local leaders, schools, families, and youth groups, we're not just building stronger communities - we're creating a model for transformative change.

Our Vission

Our vision is to extend our proven approaches to vulnerable communities worldwide, with a special focus on advancing women's rights and creating a world where every girl and woman can reach her full potential.
